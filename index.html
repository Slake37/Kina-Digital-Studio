Meet the Moldovan Family! First, thank your for taking the time to visit our website. We are Alin and Mia Moldovan, founders and owners of Kina Digital Studio. Husband and wife, who are passionate about photography, digital world and technology, web design and web development. We united our forces and came together with our amazing project. At Kina Digital Studio, our goal is to focus on client needs and meet them! To create what they want or wish and transform them in reality, to achieve and express their maximum potential. To show their beauty, strength and success! We offer digital services such as web development, web design, website consultancy, social and private events photography, business/product photography advertising photography and private medical photography (birth photography). We are based in the heart of Newcastle-upon-Tyne, Tyne and Wear but we can travel for projects all around UK. Our ambition came from our passions. We found what we loved and we want to share it with you.
